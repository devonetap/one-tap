## 🐛 Common Issues & Fixes

**GUI doesn't appear after executing**
→ Join the server fully and wait 5–10 seconds before executing. Triggering the script during the loading screen prevents the GUI from injecting correctly.

**Silent Aim fires but doesn't hit targets**
→ Your FOV radius may be set too low. Open the GUI and increase the FOV circle size until targets fall within range.

**Script works on PC but not on mobile**
→ Ensure your Android executor is fully updated. Outdated versions of Arceus X or Delta frequently lose Roblox compatibility after patches.

**Auto Fire triggers on teammates**
→ Enable the "Team Check" toggle in the GUI settings to exclude allies from the targeting system.

**Executor crashes immediately after attaching**
→ Close all other background applications and disable your antivirus temporarily — some AV tools flag executor processes and forcibly close them.

**Hitbox Expander not registering hits on fast-moving targets**
→ Combine Hitbox Expander with Triggerbot for maximum reliability against players using movement abilities.

**Script stops working mid-match**
→ A server-side hotfix may have been pushed during your session. Rejoin and re-execute with the latest version from this repository.
